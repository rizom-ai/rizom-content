# Workshop

## Cap
The workshop

## Cap Note
— thirty years of research, one consistent finding

## Headline
One session. A map your whole team *can act on*.

## Intro
We map your team's transactive memory system. Peer-reviewed studies put high-performing teams above 70% on TMS measures; most teams start well below. The gap is structural, not personal.

## Steps

### Step 1

#### Title
Survey

#### Lead
A short async survey.

#### Text
A questionnaire maps your team's coordination patterns — the workshop starts where the data leaves off.

### Step 2

#### Title
Workshop

#### Lead
A facilitated half-day in the room.

#### Text
Your team builds a shared map of expertise, decision authority, and information flow. Everybody sees the same picture — often for the first time ever.

### Step 3

#### Title
Playbook

#### Lead
A diagnostic report and a thirty-day playbook.

#### Text
Concrete changes to roles, rituals, and tooling — including how AI fits in without making the confusion worse.
