---
title: Ecosystem Architecture
---
Ecosystem architecture is presented as Rizom’s core practice for improving communities and organizations. It is described as a regenerative approach that builds diverse, resilient, effective groups around shared knowledge rather than physical proximity. The practice focuses on designing conditions where people, organizations, and networks can coordinate more effectively. Rizom applies this through curating meaningful connections, moderating shared spaces, and facilitating personal development. The goal is to remove organizational blockages that prevent collaboration and unlock new possibilities. This concept provides the broadest framework for Rizom’s consulting work and open-source tools.
