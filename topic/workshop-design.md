---
title: Workshop Design
---
The content identifies workshop design as a weakness and a threat area, especially when facilitation-heavy work is required. It cautions against assuming workshop planning can be handled alone and recommends keeping plans simple or bringing in stronger facilitation support when outcomes matter. The notes also stress that workshop work is not self-contained and may require extra preparation to land well. This suggests a need for careful scope control and practical support. The topic matters because weak facilitation can undermine the effectiveness of otherwise solid content or strategy work.
