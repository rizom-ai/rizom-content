---
title: Website Landing Page
---
A website landing page is the primary entry point for a site, designed to present an organization’s identity, value proposition, and desired next step. It should be customized with a clear narrative, mission, and call to action while reflecting the broader site structure and messaging. Effective landing pages communicate value quickly, build trust, and guide visitors toward action through concise, persuasive copy and a cohesive service narrative. Because landing pages are a common weak point even in otherwise well-organized sites, they often benefit from focused testing and iterative refinement on a high-stakes page to determine the structure that best supports engagement and conversion.
