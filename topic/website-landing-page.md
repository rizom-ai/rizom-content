---
title: Website Landing Page
---
The welcome page content positions the site as an organization home page that should be customized with a story, mission, or call to action. It emphasizes that the displayed experience is generated from the brain's content, including both the page itself and the navigation structure. The text encourages editing the page to introduce the organization and to shape the public-facing presence. This makes the landing page a central entry point for defining identity and tone. It is a reusable topic because many deployments will need a customizable homepage that reflects the organization's message.
