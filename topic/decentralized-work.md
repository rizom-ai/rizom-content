---
title: Decentralized Work
---
The article describes emerging systems that allow people to coordinate outside traditional institutions while retaining autonomy. These systems are distinguished from ordinary remote work or gig platforms because they aim to create new social and economic structures. AI, remote collaboration, creator platforms, and blockchain are presented as enabling infrastructure for this shift. The model emphasizes coordination without centralized control, ownership over employment, and network formation based on shared values. Geography becomes less important in determining access to opportunity and compensation. Rizom is presented as one implementation of this broader vision for professional collaboration.
