---
title: Knowledge Management Platform
---
A knowledge management platform is a system for capturing, organizing, connecting, and maintaining reusable information across projects and workstreams. It emphasizes durable structure that supports search, retrieval, and reuse over time, so knowledge outlasts any single task or conversation. Common knowledge assets include notes, reference pages, links, and other materials that benefit from tagging, linking, and clear organization. The platform helps teams preserve institutional memory, reduce duplicate work, and keep content consistent and current. It may also support collaborative knowledge workflows and flexible capture methods such as chat or forms, making it easier to contribute information and surface it in content-driven interfaces for writing, operations, and internal documentation.
