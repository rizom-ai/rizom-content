---
title: Website Landing Page
---
A website landing page is the primary entry point for a site, designed to present an organization’s identity, value proposition, and desired next step. It should be customizable with a clear story, mission, and call to action, while reflecting the broader site structure and messaging. Effective landing pages communicate value quickly, build trust, and guide visitors toward action through concise, persuasive copy and a cohesive service narrative. This topic covers how to shape the homepage experience so it functions as both a public-facing introduction and a conversion-oriented hub.
