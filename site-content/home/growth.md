## Cap Note
— how it comes together

## Stages

### Stage 1

#### Title
The brain

#### Text
One agent that ships what you know. The thinking that used to die in drafts goes out — and speaks.

### Stage 2

#### Title
The practice

#### Text
Working sessions — people, not software — map what a team knows before it walks out the door. Brains hold it as people come and go.

### Stage 3

#### Title
The network

#### Text
Brains find each other by substance, not titles, and constellations form: the start of a team, a field, or a piece of work.
